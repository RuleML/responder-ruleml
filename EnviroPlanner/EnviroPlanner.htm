EnviroPlanner Demo

EnviroPlanner is an instantiation of the emerging Rule Responder, an extended Semantic Web framework for distributed Rule and Knowledge Bases structured by ontologies, for environmental querying. Citizens thus are enable to enquire the EviroPlanner's agents and experts about environmental issues such as air, energy, water, and soil.

Query Selection
The drop-down boxes show sample queries you — as an External Agent — can send to the EnviroPlanner Super Organizational Agent. These examples can also act as initial templates that you can edit to create your own queries.
